Our Story

Our journey in providing the best solutions for water treatment.

The journey of PT Bayu Surya Bakti Construction began with a creative idea from our founder, Mr. Ir. I Nyoman Yasanegara, who has been involved in the water treatment sector for nearly 20 years. Seeing the increasing issues of water scarcity and pollution in Indonesia, he was inspired to establish a company that could make a positive contribution in the field of water management in Indonesia.
