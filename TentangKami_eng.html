The journey of PT Bayu Surya Bakti Construction began with the visionary idea of our founder, Mr. Ir. I Nyoman Yasa Negara, who has been involved in the water treatment industry for approximately 20 years. Recognizing the growing challenges of water scarcity and pollution in Indonesia, he was inspired to establish a company that could contribute positively to water management in Indonesia.

The company was founded in 2014 with a small team of dedicated professionals. Over the years, we have successfully completed various water and wastewater treatment projects across Indonesia. Our portfolio includes advanced solutions for water filtration, industrial wastewater treatment, and recycled water treatment. With every project, we strive to raise standards of environmental responsibility and efficiency in water management.

In our commitment to staying at the forefront of water treatment technology, PT BSBK has made significant investments in research and development. Our R&D initiatives focus on exploring new methods, materials, and processes to enhance the efficiency and sustainability of our projects.

Through continuous learning and collaboration with industry experts, PT BSBK remains committed to pioneering advancements in water and wastewater treatment technology, creating lasting positive impacts on the environment and society.
